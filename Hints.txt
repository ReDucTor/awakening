Awakening Hints

When packing, just because things are in the same box, doesn’t make them the same.

When unpacking, you don’t put everything in the same place.

I’ve got 199 problems but I’ll just salt my wounds.

First place is always treated differently than the losers.

Unfamiliar logic may appear as randomness.

Details are important but don’t miss the big picture.

Nobody is above the law, we all have to follow the same rules.

We all have something to hide.

Finally, in order to realize authentic awakening, you must be moved by the Dharma.
